I am a third year CS Master student at Norhtwestern Polytechnical University and member of the National Engineering Laboratory for Integrated Aero-Space-Ground-Ocean Big Data Application Technology, advised by Prof. Zhang Yanning and Co-advised by Pro. Yang JiaQi. My research interests are in developing reliable computer vision systems that can be deployed in the real world. Specifically, I am interested 3D vsion including point cloud registration, feature matching using local descriptors, 3D image rendering and Object detection using deep learning and traditional approaches. I earned my Bachelor in Software Engineering in Spring 2019, at Zhejiang Normal University, where I was advised by Prof. Zheng ZhongLong on developing models to run on a self-driving cars simulator.
